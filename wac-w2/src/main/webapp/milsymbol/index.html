  • Visual Properties
  • Text Modifiers
C   Quantity:   (9 characters) A text modifier in an equipment symbol that identifies the number of items present.
F   Reinforced or Reduced:   (3 Characters) A text modifier in a unit symbol that displays (+) for reinforced, (-) for reduced, (±) reinforced and reduced..
G   Staff Comments:   (20 Characters) A text modifier for units, equipment and installations; content is implementation specific.
H   Additional Information:   (20 Characters) A text modifier for units, equipment, and installations; content is implementation specific.
J   Evaluation Rating:   (2 Characters) A text modifier for units, equipment, and installations that consists of a one-letter reliability rating and a one-number credibility rating.
K   Combat Effectiveness:   (5 Characters) A text modifier for units and installations that indicates unit effectiveness or installation capability.
L   Signature Equipment:   (1 Characters) A text modifier for hostile equipment; "!" indicates detectable electronic signatures.
M   Higher Formation:   (21 Characters) A text modifier for units that indicates number or title of higher echelon command (corps are designated by Roman numerals).
N   Hostile:   (3 Characters) A text modifier for equipment; letters "ENY" denote hostile symbols.
P   IFF/SIF:   (5 Characters) A text modifier displaying IFF/SIF Identification modes and codes.
Q   Direction (degrees):   (4 Characters) A graphic modifier for units and equipment that identifies the direction of movement or intended movement of an object.
T   Unique Designation:   (21 Characters) A text modifier for units, equipment, and installations that uniquely identifies a particular symbol or track number. Identifies acquisitions number when used with SIGINT symbology.
V   Type:   (24 Characters) A text modifier for equipment that indicates types of equipment.
W   DTG:   (16 Characters) A text modifier for units, equipment, and installations that displays DTG format: DDHHMMSSZMONYYYY or "O/O" for on order (see 5.5.2.6).
X   Altitude/Depth:   (14 Characters) A text modifier for units, equipment, and installations, that displays either altitude flight level, depth for submerged objects; or height of equipment or structures on the ground.
Y   Location:   (19 Characters) A text modifier for units, equipment, and installations that displays a symbol's location in degrees, minutes, and seconds (or in UTM or other applicable display format).
Z   Speed:   (8 Characters) A text modifier for units and equipment that displays velocity as set forth inMIL-STD-6040.
AA   Special Headquarters:   (9 Characters) A text modifier for units; indicator is contained inside the frame; contains the name of the special C2 Headquarters.
